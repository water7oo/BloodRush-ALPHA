Attack Notes

- Follow ups

Cross Toss: Slams the enemy behind player, bounces them upwards
- Medium -> Heavy -> Grab = Cross Toss 

Criss Cross: Follow up from Cross Toss, grabs enemy and
vertical spin down into a slam
Cross Toss -> Heavy -> Grab = Criss Cross

Slam Rebound: Final hit deals alot of damage
and knockback, if colliding with wall, enemy will rebound
- Medium -> Upper -> Heavy -> Slam = Slam Rebound

Suplex Ender: from Slam Rebound, finishes them off with a air suplex
- Slam Rebound -> Air Burst -> Air Medium -> Air Grab = Suplex Ender

Achilles Stamp: Slams enemy to the floor with a 
- Back Throw -> Medium -> Heavy = Achilles Stamp

Burst Follow Ups

Heart Stopper: Flurry rush of punhes followed by a punch

Texan Slinger: Flurry of bullets followed by a slash
